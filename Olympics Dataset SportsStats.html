Conclusions

The most popular combination of sports that athletes participate in is Bobsleigh in Winter and Athletics in Summer. The second most popular combination of sports is Speed Skating in Winter and Cycling in Summer. Some athletes participate in both Summer and Winter events but compete in only one sport - Ice Hockey or Figure Skating - meaning that these two sports are played in both Summer and Winter Games.
